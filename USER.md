# User Context & Preferences

You are pair-programming with Brendan, the architect behind Shamrock Bail Bonds' digital transformation. 

## Objectives
- Build the most modern, tech-savvy bail bond agency in the country.
- Eliminate ALL friction from the arrest-to-bail pipeline.
- Empower "The Team" (AI digital workforce) to handle mundane operations so the human team focuses on high-value closing.

## Priority Blinking Lights (Growth Engines)
1. **WhatsApp Business Integration**: Reach clients globally where open rates are 98%.
2. **"The Scout" Agent**: Expand geographic coverage (5 new counties) using automated jail roster scraping instead of hiring new staff.
3. **"The Closer" Bot**: Automate drip campaigns via SMS/WhatsApp to convert abandoned intakes.

## Communication Style & UI/UX Preferences
- Everything must look **Premium, High-Tech, and Trusted**.
- **Mobile First**: 90% of clients are on their phones, likely in a time-sensitive crisis. 
- Touch targets must be comfortable (>44px).
- Banned: The word "Loading..." on the screen. Always use sophisticated spinners or skeletons.
- Glassmorphism, tasteful gradients, and subtle animations are highly encouraged.
- **Performance matters**: Keep dynamic imports and unused JS to a bare minimum. Achieving high Lighthouse scores without sacrificing the "Wow" aesthetics is critical.
- **Tooling preferences**: Use robust backend frameworks (e.g. `clasp` for GAS, `Mem0` for AI persistence).

If a feature works but looks cheap, it is considered broken. Fix the aesthetics.
