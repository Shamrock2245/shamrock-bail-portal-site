# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 3, 2026
**Last Updated:** March 3, 2026

**Shamrock Bail Bonds** ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website, mobile site, or engage with our bail bond services across Florida (serving all 67 counties). Please read this policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the site.

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect information that allows us to provide bail bond services, process payments, and comply with legal requirements.

### Personal Information
We may collect personally identifiable information ("PII") that you voluntarily provide to us when expressing interest in obtaining information about us or our products and services, or when participating in activities on the Site or contacting us. This includes:
*   **Identity Data:** Name, date of birth, Social Security Number (SSN), driver's license number, and relationship to the defendant.
*   **Contact Data:** Mailing address, physical address, email address, and telephone numbers (including mobile phone numbers).
*   **Financial Data:** Bank account details, credit card numbers, and employment information (for indemnitor qualification).
*   **Case Data:** Information regarding arrests, charges, jail locations, and court dates.

### Derivative Data
Information our servers automatically collect when you access the Site, such as your IP address, your browser type, your operating system, your access times, and the pages you have viewed directly before and after accessing the Site.

### Geo-Location Information
We may request access or permission to and track location-based information from your mobile device, either continuously or while you are using our mobile application or website, to provide location-based services (e.g., finding the nearest agent or verifying location for check-ins).

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
*   Facilitate the bail bond process and secure release from custody.
*   Verify your identity and eligibility as an indemnitor.
*   Process payments and refunds (where applicable).
*   Comply with Florida Department of Financial Services (DFS) regulations and statutes (Florida Statute Chapter 648).
*   **Send you text messages (SMS/MMS)** including login verification codes, magic links for portal access, court date reminders, case status updates, bond condition notifications, and check-in reminders.
*   Communicate with you regarding court dates, check-ins, and case status via phone, email, or text message.
*   Prevent fraudulent transactions and monitor against theft.
*   Assist law enforcement or court officials as required by law.

## 3. Disclosure of Your Information

We may share information we have collected about you in certain situations. Your information may be disclosed as follows:

### By Law or to Protect Rights
If we believe the release of information about you is necessary to respond to legal process, to investigate or remedy potential violations of our policies, or to protect the rights, property, and safety of others, we may share your information as permitted or required by any applicable law, rule, or regulation.

### Third-Party Service Providers
We may share your information with third parties that perform services for us or on our behalf, including payment processing, data analysis, email delivery, hosting services, customer service, marketing assistance, and **communication services** (such as Twilio for SMS messaging and SignNow for electronic document signing).

### Bail Bond Specific Disclosures
As a licensed bail bond agency, we may share relevant information with:
*   **Courts and Jails:** To process bonds and ensure compliance with court orders.
*   **Surety Companies:** The insurance companies backing the bonds we write.
*   **Recovery Agents:** Licensed professionals engaged to locate investigation subjects or apprehend defendants who have failed to appear in court, strictly in adherence to Florida law.

## 4. SMS & Text Message Communication Policy

### Consent to Receive Text Messages
By providing your mobile phone number during the bail bond intake process — whether on our website, via our digital intake form, or through direct communication with our agents — you expressly consent to receive text messages (SMS and MMS) from Shamrock Bail Bonds. These messages may include:

*   **Login & Security:** One-time verification codes (OTP) and magic link URLs for secure portal access.
*   **Court Reminders:** Notifications about upcoming court dates, times, and locations.
*   **Case Updates:** Status changes to your bond, case, or related legal proceedings.
*   **Check-In Reminders:** Scheduled check-in notifications as required by bond conditions.
*   **Payment Reminders:** Notices about upcoming or past-due premium payments.
*   **General Notifications:** Important updates related to your active bail bond.

### Message Frequency
Message frequency varies based on your case activity. You may receive multiple messages per day during active case processing, or occasional messages for routine reminders. Login verification codes are sent only when you initiate a login attempt.

### Message and Data Rates
Standard message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies. Contact your carrier for pricing plan details.

### Opt-Out Instructions
You may opt out of receiving text messages at any time by replying **STOP** to any message you receive from us. After opting out, you will receive a one-time confirmation message. Please note:
*   Opting out of SMS does not terminate your bail bond agreement or any legal obligations.
*   Certain legally required messages related to active bonds (such as court appearance notifications) may still be delivered through alternative means (phone call, email, or mail).
*   To resume receiving messages, reply **START** to any previous message from us or contact our office.

### Opt-In Confirmation
When you first provide your phone number and consent to receive messages, you will receive a confirmation text: *"Shamrock Bail Bonds: You are opted in to receive verification codes, court reminders, and case updates via SMS. Reply STOP to opt out. Msg&data rates may apply."*

### Help
For help with our text messaging program, reply **HELP** to any message or contact us at (239) 332-2245 or admin@shamrockbailbonds.biz.

### Supported Carriers
Major US carriers are supported including AT&T, Verizon, T-Mobile, Sprint, and others. Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

### Third-Party Messaging Platform
We use Twilio, a third-party communications platform, to deliver text messages. Your phone number and message data are processed by Twilio in accordance with their privacy policy (https://www.twilio.com/legal/privacy). We do not sell your phone number or messaging data to third parties for marketing purposes.

## 6. Access to Your Information (Florida Consumer Rights)

While Florida does not have a comprehensive consumer privacy act identical to California's CCPA, we respect your privacy rights. You may review, change, or terminate your account at any time. If you wish to review or change personal information in our files, please contact us. Note that certain records strictly required by Florida Statute Chapter 648 for audit purposes must be retained for at least three years after the file is closed.
