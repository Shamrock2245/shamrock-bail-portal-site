# Shamrock Bail Bonds — Developer & Client Documentation

Welcome to the official documentation for **Shamrock Bail Bonds**, serving **Lee County, Charlotte County, Collier County, and all 65 counties in Florida**.

This portal exists to explain how our digital bail bond system works for:

- **Defendants** (applying, waivers, weekly check-ins)
- **Indemnitors / Cosignors** (financial forms, collateral, payments, signatures)
- **Shamrock Staff** (case creation, monitoring, exporting packets)

---

## Why Shamrock Bail Bonds?

- Over **13 years of trusted service** in Southwest Florida.
- Licensed to post bail bonds in **all 65 Florida counties**.
- **Fast release**: The cost of a bond is set by the state at **10% of the bail amount or $100 per charge, whichever is greater**.
- **Collateral**: In most cases (99%), only signatures are required. For bonds over $50,000, property owners may be required as indemnitors.
- **Check-Ins**: Defendants check in weekly (usually Wednesdays) using GPS + photo verification.

---

## How to Use This Portal

- **Defendants**:  
  Start by logging in with your secure magic link. You’ll complete your application, sign waivers, and check in weekly.

- **Indemnitors / Cosignors**:  
  You’ll fill out a financial indemnity form, optionally pledge collateral, authorize payments, and sign required documents.

- **Staff**:  
  Use the console to create cases, prefill forms, track progress, resend links, and export completed packets.

---

## Counties We Serve

- **Primary focus**: Lee, Charlotte, and Collier Counties.
- **All other counties**: We can post bonds anywhere in Florida, with a **$100 base transfer fee** (additional fees vary by local bondsman availability).
