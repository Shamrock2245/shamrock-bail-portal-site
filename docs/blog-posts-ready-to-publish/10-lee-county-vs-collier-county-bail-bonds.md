## Lee County: Fort Myers and Cape Coral

### The Jail
**Lee County Jail**  
2115 Main Street, Fort Myers, FL 33901  
Phone: (239) 477-1000  
Operated by the Lee County Sheriff's Office

### First Appearance Hearings
Lee County typically holds First Appearance hearings **twice daily at 8:30 AM and 1:30 PM** at the Lee County Justice Center. Hearings are livestreamed — Shamrock monitors the livestream and posts bonds as bond amounts are announced.

### Bail Schedule
Lee County uses a standard misdemeanor bail schedule that allows release without a First Appearance for many common offenses. For felonies, a judge sets bail at the hearing.

### Release Processing Time
- Average after bond posted: **4–8 hours**
- Lee County is one of the faster-processing jails in SWFL
- Shamrock's office is **0.3 miles from the jail** — the closest bondsman to the Lee County Jail entrance

### Local Proximity Advantage
Our office at **1528 Broadway, Fort Myers** is directly across from the Lee County Justice Center. Our bondsmen have established relationships with the booking staff. This translates to measurably faster processing.

## Collier County: Naples and Marco Island

### The Jail
**Collier County Jail**  
3301 Tamiami Trail East, Naples, FL 34112  
Phone: (239) 252-9300  
Operated by the Collier County Sheriff's Office

### First Appearance Hearings
Collier County typically holds First Appearance hearings **once daily, typically at 8:30 AM**. Unlike Lee County, there is generally only one hearing per day, which means an arrest on a Tuesday afternoon may not have its First Appearance until Wednesday morning.

**What this means for families:** If your loved one was arrested in Collier County after the daily First Appearance hearing has concluded, they may remain in jail until the following morning before a bond amount is set for felony charges.

### Release Processing Time
- Average after bond posted: **4–8 hours**
- Processing speed is comparable to Lee County for most situations
- Weekends may see extended processing times

### How Shamrock Handles Collier County Bonds
Shamrock processes all Collier County bonds fully remotely — paperwork by text, payment by phone. We monitor the Collier County docket and alert families the moment a bond amount is confirmed at First Appearance.
